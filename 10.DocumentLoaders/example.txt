Cricket

Introduction
Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 11 players each. 
It originated in England and is now played worldwide, especially in countries like India, Australia, England, Pakistan, and South Africa.

Basic Rules
- A match is played on a 22-yard pitch with wickets at both ends.
- One team bats while the other team bowls/fields.
- The batting team tries to score runs, while the bowling team tries to get them out.
- Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between the wickets or by hitting boundaries (4s and 6s).

Formats of the Game
1. Test Cricket: Played over 5 days with unlimited overs.
2. One Day International (ODI): Each team faces 50 overs.
3. Twenty20 (T20): Each team faces 20 overs, the shortest and most exciting format.

Famous Tournaments
- ICC Cricket World Cup
- ICC T20 World Cup
- Indian Premier League (IPL)
- The Ashes (England vs Australia)

Famous Players
- Sachin Tendulkar (India)
- Sir Don Bradman (Australia)
- Virat Kohli (India)
- AB de Villiers (South Africa)
